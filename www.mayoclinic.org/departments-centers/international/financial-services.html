Financial Services

Whenever possible, Mayo Clinic tries to remove financial considerations from providing medical care. We have direct payment contracts with a variety of international insurers. A dedicated staff of international account representatives can assist patients and families with financial details before, during and after their visit to Mayo Clinic.

Services include:

  • Information about deposits and payment
  • Fee estimates and fee updates for medical services ordered by a Mayo physician
  • Mayo Clinic's cost estimator tool

Mayo Clinic strives to provide timely, accurate and concise billing services to patients and their families. Our staff can assist you with any financial and insurance concerns that may arise.
