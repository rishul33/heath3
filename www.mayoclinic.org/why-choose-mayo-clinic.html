Why Choose Mayo Clinic?

Why choose Mayo Clinic?

When you're facing a serious illness, we stand by your side.

Learn more about current appointment availability and safe face-to-face care at Mayo Clinic .

  • Is Mayo Clinic for me?

    The answer is yes for 1.3 million patients from nearly 130 countries every year. The reason is simple: Compassion is at the heart of our care. At Mayo Clinic, unhurried, comprehensive evaluations offer the best chance of healing and getting back to your life.

    See what our patients have to say

  • What makes care at Mayo Clinic different?

    Effective treatment depends on getting the right diagnosis as soon as possible. Our specialists collaborate across disciplines to listen to your story, evaluate your condition from every angle, and develop a diagnosis and treatment plan that's just for you.

    See how the process works

  • Does Mayo Clinic treat my condition?

    Mayo Clinic experts solve the world's toughest medical problems — one patient at a time. No matter what serious, complex or rare health challenge you're facing, you can be confident that Mayo Clinic has extensive experience in treating patients with your specific condition.

    Learn more about our expertise

  • Is Mayo Clinic doing research on my condition?

    Our relentless research yields scientific discoveries and innovations that reach our patients faster and help them heal sooner. Our patients are frequently among the first to benefit from breakthrough therapies, advanced technologies and clinical research trials.

    Learn more about research at Mayo Clinic
